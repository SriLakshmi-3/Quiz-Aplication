About Online Quiz Application

Welcome to the Online Quiz Application! Our platform provides a convenient way for users to create, manage, and take quizzes on various subjects.

Key features of our application include:

  • Creating new quizzes with customizable questions and answers
  • Managing existing quizzes, including editing and deleting questions
  • Viewing all users registered on the platform
  • Taking quizzes and receiving instant feedback on performance

Whether you're an educator looking to create quizzes for your students or a learner eager to test your knowledge, our platform offers a seamless experience for all.
